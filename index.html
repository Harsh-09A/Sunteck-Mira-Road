Sunteck Mira Road

Experience tranquil living at Sunteck Mira Road, a residential complex set under the backdrop of lush hills, creek, and the sea coast. Its 2 and 3 BHK apartments offer enchanting mountain and ocean views. You also get access to fabulous amenities such as a gym and clubhouse spread across 1.5 lakh sq. ft. of land. There is a Ganesha, Shiv, and Jain temple within the premises as well. The locale of Mira Road further gives you access to the top schools and hospitals such as Bright International School & Global Multispeciality Hospital. The business hubs across the city are also not far away as the Western Express Highway & Mira Road Railway Station are within 10 to 15 minutes. With charming city lanes on one side and the natural beauty of this locale on the other - Sunteck Mira Road can be your perfect retreat from the hustle-bustle of the city.
